Climate Change

This website is demonstrates the issues of climate change and what we need to do about it

Climate change is a topic that is talked about a lot during the current society, however, it is important that we know the severity of the issue, rather than not acknoledging its existence.

This website aims to educate about the change of climate change from the past, present, and future, while providing explanitory information about myself

Climate change action is crucial because rising temperatures, sea levels, and extreme weather already threaten ecosystems, economies, and human health worldwide. Without urgent action, billions face food insecurity, displacement, and worsening disasters. Reducing emissions and protecting vulnerable communities now is essential to safeguard a livable future for generations to come.
